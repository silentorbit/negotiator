# Privacy Policy

We at Silent Orbit don't give a shit about your privacy, we couldn't care less about your data.

Therefore we never see any of your data.

Our software and extensions may collect all your most sensitive secrets, but that data is only stored locally, on your machine, an never leaves the extension.

If you don't like this you can perform the following steps.

1. Open the extensions page chrome://extensions/
2. Find the Negotiator extension and click: "Inspect views _service worker_"
3. In the left menu, under "Storage" click to expand "Extension storage" and then "Session"
4. Click the value and copy
5. Go to [pastebin](https://pastebin.com/)
6. Past the content
7. Click submit
